Rise of Orpheus


When Orpheus lost Eurydice to the Underworld, he vowed to bring her back – not with force, but with song. In Rise of Orpheus, players join the mythic hero as he descends into the shadowy depths in search of his lost love. As their paths cross again, ethereal forces stir. Each time they reunite on the reels, their love reshapes the game world – wilds emerge, multipliers grow, and their shared presence becomes a beacon through the gloom.

But Hades does not relinquish easily. With Cerberus snapping at their heels and a world of shadows pressing in, can Orpheus reach Eurydice in time... and bring her back to the light?
