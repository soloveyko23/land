Chicken Road 2.0 — Coming Soon!

Get ready to cross the road… again, but this time with even more thrills! Chicken Road 2.0 is the next evolution of the beloved arcade adventure, where you guide a fearless chicken across endless roads, rivers, and all-new traps.

Brace Yourself: Chicken Road 2.0 Is Almost Here!

Get ready for fast-paced chaos in Chicken Road 2.0 — a hilariously addictive arcade game where your mission is simple: guide a bold little chicken across endless roads, rivers, train tracks, and unpredictable terrain without getting flattened, soaked, or stranded.

Think it's easy? Think again.

In Chicken Road 2.0 , hazards strike from every direction. Cars zoom past without warning, logs float just out of reach, and surprise trains barrel through the landscape. Your only tools? Sharp reflexes, quick thinking, and a bit of luck. Make one wrong move, and it's game over — but trust us, you'll be eager to try again.

More than just a sequel, this is a complete overhaul. With revamped graphics, smoother animations, richer environments, and a fresh dynamic soundtrack, Chicken Road 2.0 takes everything you loved and dials it up. Explore a variety of vibrant zones — from villages and snowy paths to riversides and surreal dreamscapes — each filled with unique challenges.

What's New in Chicken Road 2.0:
• Totally reimagined visuals – Bright, colorful, and buttery-smooth across all devices.
• Smarter, dynamic obstacles – Shifting lanes, collapsing bridges, and more surprises.
• Secret zones & bonus areas – Discover hidden routes and bonus rewards.
• Unlockable characters – New chickens with unique styles and even special movement traits.
• Endless variety – No run is ever the same. React, adapt, and outsmart the chaos.

Your goal? Get as far as you can, beat your high score, and just maybe uncover the truth behind that age-old question: why did the chicken cross the road?

Whether you're looking for a quick challenge or eyeing the top of the leaderboard, Chicken Road 2.0 is the perfect pick-up-and-play experience — ideal for short breaks, commutes, or anytime you’re up for a fast-paced reflex test.

Launching soon on all major platforms! In the meantime, check out the official gameplay preview for a sneak peek at the chaos, characters, and environments that await.

Get ready to tap, dodge, hop, and slide your way through the most ridiculous traffic jam in mobile game history.

Because crossing the road has never been this dangerous… or this fun.
