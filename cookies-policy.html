Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences, enhance your experience, and help us improve our services.

Cookies may be “session cookies” (which are deleted once you close your browser) or “persistent cookies” (which remain on your device until they expire or you delete them).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:
• Essential cookies – to ensure that the website functions properly.
• Preference cookies – to remember your settings and improve your user experience.
• Analytics cookies – to understand how users interact with the site and to improve performance.
• Third-party cookies – used by services such as Google or embedded content (like games or videos) to track usage or provide functionality.

These cookies do not usually collect information that directly identifies you, but they help us provide a better and more personalized experience.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party tools such as Google Analytics, which set cookies on your device to collect anonymous usage statistics. These services may also use cookies to deliver targeted ads or content.

Please refer to their own privacy and cookie policies for more details:
• Google: https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to:
• View what cookies are stored
• Delete all or specific cookies
• Block cookies from certain websites
• Set preferences for certain types of cookies

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website and your overall experience.

For more information, visit:
• www.allaboutcookies.org
• www.youronlinechoices.eu
